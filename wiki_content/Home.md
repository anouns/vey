# 🌌 VEY.AI - Knowledge OS

Welcome to the **VEY.AI** Wiki! This documentation is designed to help you get the most out of your personal Knowledge Operating System.

## What is VEY.AI?

**VEY.AI** is a desktop application that transforms your local files, books, and code into a single, intelligent knowledge base. By pointing the app to a folder, you enable an on-device AI to analyze your data and answer questions with strict grounding in your own documents.

### Key Highlights
- **100% Privacy**: All processing happens locally. No data is sent to the cloud unless you explicitly choose an external AI provider.
- **RAG Powered**: Uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation to ensure accuracy and cite sources.
- **Developer First**: Built with **Tauri**, **React**, and **FastAPI**, offering a high-performance terminal-like experience for your files.
